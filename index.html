Akan Name Calculator

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana,
children are given their first name as 'day name' which corresponds to
the day in the week they werre born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
